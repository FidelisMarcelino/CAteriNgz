About Us

Our Story

CAteriNgz began in 2010 in a small kitchen in Jakarta, where our founder Maya Santoso turned her passion for cooking into a beloved catering service. What started as small gatherings for friends quickly grew into a successful business known for delicious, homemade dishes.

Expanding to Bandung in 2013 and Malang in 2015, we stayed true to our commitment to using fresh, local, and seasonal ingredients. Our chefs work closely with local farmers and artisans, ensuring every dish bursts with flavor and supports sustainable practices.

Today, CAteriNgz is a go-to for events across Jakarta, Bandung, and Malang, offering a diverse menu from traditional Indonesian cuisine to contemporary fusion dishes. We continue to innovate and bring joy to our clients through our culinary creations, making every event a memorable experience.

Our Vision

To be the top choice for unforgettable dining experiences in Jakarta, Bandung, and Malang, driven by culinary innovation and excellence.

Our Mission

S.E.R.V.E
