Our Menu

Nasi Tumpeng

Nasi Tumpeng is a traditional Indonesian dish served on special occasions as a symbol of blessings and gratitude. The contents include fried chicken, omelet, fried tofu, fried tempeh, chili sauce, cucumber and crackers.

Ayam Bakar

Ayam Bakar is a traditional Indonesian dish consisting of pieces of chicken seasoned and grilled until cooked, often served with white rice and fresh vegetables.

Fried Rice

Fried rice is a flavorful Asian dish made by stir-frying pre-cooked rice with a mix of ingredients like vegetables, eggs, and meat.

Chicken Satay

Chicken Satay is an Indonesian dish consisting of skewered and grilled chicken pieces, often served with peanut sauce as a dipping sauce.

Gudeg

Gudeg is a traditional Javanese dish made from young jackfruit stewed with palm sugar, coconut milk, and various spices, typically served with rice.
